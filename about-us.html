What is Diabetes?

“Website title” is a diabetes management platform developed by a team of students from the University of Melbourne. Features include:
- Easy to use blood glucose tracking
- Customisable health tracing
- Useful presentation of data for pattern finding and effective management
- Correspondence between clinicians and patients
- Secure data storage
- Various tools for clinicians, including: a user friendly dashboard with access to patient data and comments
To use the diabetes management features: sign up, or if you already have an account: log in. Alternatively, see our “About Diabetes” page.
